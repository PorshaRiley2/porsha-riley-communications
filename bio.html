Press Bio

Porsha Riley is a communications professional with experience in government communications, journalism and public affairs.

She most recently served as communications director at the Oklahoma Medical Marijuana Authority, the nation’s largest state-regulated medical cannabis program.

Prior to that role, she worked as a broadcast journalist in Oklahoma City television newsrooms, where she covered the COVID-19 pandemic, major weather events, state and local politics and breaking news. She served as a political reporter and nightside reporter and produced investigative reporting on public policy and accountability.

Riley is a mother and spends time with her son. They enjoy local restaurants, including burger restaurants across the state, as well as movies, travel and trying new food establishments. She also spends time with her dog.
