## Expertise

Porsha Riley provides communications strategy and advisory support grounded in experience leading statewide public information efforts and broadcast journalism.

Her work focuses on:
- Crisis communications and rapid response messaging  
- Executive communications and strategic advisory  
- Media relations and reputation management  
- Public information strategy in regulated environments  
- Stakeholder and interagency communications  

## Background

She most recently served as Communications Director and public relations lead at the Oklahoma Medical Marijuana Authority, where she led statewide communications strategy, including media relations, crisis communications, stakeholder engagement and executive messaging, and served as senior advisor to executive leadership.

Prior to that, she worked as a broadcast journalist covering breaking news, major weather events, politics and the COVID-19 pandemic, including roles with KOCO 5 News, KAKE News and News On 6.
