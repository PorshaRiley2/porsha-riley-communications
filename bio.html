Bio

Porsha Riley is a senior communications leader with experience in government communications, journalism and public affairs in regulated environments.

She most recently served as communications director and public relations lead at the Oklahoma Medical Marijuana Authority, the nation’s largest state-regulated medical cannabis program. She led statewide communications strategy and served as senior advisor to executive leadership.

Before that role, she was a broadcast journalist covering breaking news, major weather events, politics and COVID-19.

In her free time, she enjoys traveling with her son and exploring restaurants across different states.
