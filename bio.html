Press Bio

Porsha Riley is a senior communications leader with experience in government communications, journalism and public affairs across high-visibility, regulated environments.

She most recently served as Communications Director and public relations lead at the Oklahoma Medical Marijuana Authority, the nation’s largest state-regulated medical cannabis program. In this role, she led statewide communications strategy, including media relations, crisis communications, stakeholder engagement and executive messaging, and served as senior advisor to executive leadership.

Prior to that role, she worked as a broadcast journalist covering breaking news, major weather events, politics and the COVID-19 pandemic across Oklahoma media markets.

In her free time, she enjoys traveling with her son and exploring restaurants across different states.
